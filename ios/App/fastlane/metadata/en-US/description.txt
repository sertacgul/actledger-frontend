ActLedger is an end-to-end platform for managing your enterprise operations from a single hub. With 15+ industry-specific KPI templates, AI-powered analytics, and real-time field tracking, ActLedger helps you achieve operational excellence.

KEY FEATURES

- Real-Time Operations: Field data is collected, processed, and reflected on the cockpit screen instantly.
- OperIQ - AI-Powered Insights: Our AI engine analyzes all field data, generates risk and opportunity signals, and recommends actions.
- Enterprise KPI Engine: 7800+ ready-made KPI templates with a 5-layer measurement structure. Industry-specific metrics activated with a single tap.
- Field Operations: Mobile-friendly task management, location-based tracking, and offline support.
- Role-Based Access: 8-level permission hierarchy, department-level data isolation, and audit trail.
- Real-Time Dashboard: Draggable widgets, live charts, and department-level views.
- AssetIQ Inventory Management: Instant stock movements via QR code, batch/lot tracking, hierarchical approval flows.
- Live Map and Facility Management: GPS tracking, 2D/3D facility plans, IoT device placement.
- Excel/SQL Import: Seamlessly import your existing data. Excel, CSV, and SQL database integration.

15+ INDUSTRY TEMPLATES

Manufacturing, construction, logistics, energy, healthcare, retail, agriculture, food, mining, tourism, and more. Terminology, departments, and KPI sets speak your industry's language.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Admin selects the industry and departments, builds the organization tree.
2. KPI templates are automatically distributed, teams start working immediately.
3. OperIQ generates actionable insights for decision makers.
4. Mobile QR scanning for stock operations, hierarchical approval flow, live facility map.
